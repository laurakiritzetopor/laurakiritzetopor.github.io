I’m Laura Kiritzé-Topor a Paris-born Industrial Designer now based in San Francisco. After a first Master Degree in Industrial Design at École Boulle, I specialized in Innovation, Design and Luxury obtaining a Master Degree 2 in this university.

Then I worked for 3 years at Kenzo - LVMH as an Environment and Merchandising Designer where I developed my CAD and project management skills to an advanced level. As a project manager I was in charge of the creative part and technical development on several perfumes launches. This experience provided me extensive knowledge in manufacturing practices.

A construction company awarded me for my smart door project and I also won this two days european design challenge with my friends. This gave me a strong experience in conception and team communication to take a conceptualized idea and make it a reality.

I am currently a freelance industrial designer and I'm always looking for new opportunities so don’t hesitate to say hello. I don’t like coffee but I drink a lot of green tea.
