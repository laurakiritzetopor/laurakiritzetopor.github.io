Chair A

The chair A has been designed for an everyday use and may be displayed in a public or private environnement. It aims to be robust, durable and also timeless to fit any interiors. The overall shape is quite refined, symetric and a bit archetypal to set this chair as a classic and never get weary of it.

The main characteristic of this chair is the slot system used to assemble the back and seat. A groove is cut out on the seat to welcome the back. The combination seat+back rests on two round section sticks and is maintained by four fixing screws and two hardwood cleats.
