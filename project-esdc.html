European Street Design Challenge

Les sentiers de la plaine
First prize ESDC

2012

Collaborative work: Chloé Hueber, Pauline Laufmöller-Marlier, Arthur Richard, Frédéric Valentin.

The goal of this competion was to provide to the Plaine Saint-Denis area inhabitants a solution to communicate and organize local events by themselves. «Les sentiers de la plaine» network is directly implanted in the urban space by transforming walls, paths and all the others city properties in broadcasting materials.
